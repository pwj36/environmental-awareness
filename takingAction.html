TAKING ACTION

HOW CAN WE SAVE THE ENVIRONMENT?

3Rs METHOD


One of the main methods you can save the environment is by following the 3Rs- reduce, reuse and recycle (Rinkesh, n.d.):

  1. Reduce: reduce the amount of waste generated. For example, reduce usage of disposable plates, utensils and napkins. Instead, use washable plates and utensils and handkerchiefs.

  2. Reuse: Repurpose items to use for another intended purpose. For example, old jeans and clothes can be reused as bags.

  3. Recycle: To convert waste materials into usable ones. For example, using plastic bottles as a pot to plant trees.

OTHER METHODS

Use Recycle Bags

Reduce use of plastic bags as it take very long to decompose.

Use Reusable Bottles

Use reusable water bottles instead of plastic bottles.

Volunteer

Volunteer in your community for clean-up events.

Spread Awareness

Spread awareness and encourage others to take action in protecting the environment.

Ride a Bike or Walk

Reduce the usage of automobiles as it can lead to greenhouse gas emissions.

Use Renewable Energy

Although costly, it doesn't generate greenhouse gases while producing energy and power.
