ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS


WHAT IS ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS?

Environmental awareness refers to an individual's knowledge about the environment, the influence of human actions on the environment, and the importance of environmental protection (The Pachamama Alliance, n.d.). There have been many awareness initiatives, such as the 'Go Green' campaign, encouraging people to be sensitive to the natural environment and make economically responsible decisions (Sullivan, 2022). For example, a person can go green by purchasing a glass or ceramic bottle instead of a disposable plastic bottle.


WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

The world is currently facing enormous environmental challenges. Large amounts of carbon dioxide emissions have caused pollution and climate change, which have the potential to alter our environment severely. Hence, widespread behavioural change is required to maintain a sustainable environment. Environmental awareness enables individuals to learn about the environmental problems and gives them increased exposure to nature. Love The Oceans (2020) mentioned that it is highly probable that individuals would care about a topic and make informed judgments when they know more information about it. Awareness would make it a simpler procedure to achieve sustainable living because it enables us to understand the importance of stopping behaviours that will harm the environment and implementing methods to restore the damage caused by us.
